Steps to acquire Italian Citizenship by descent

Free Online Eligibility Check

Our eligibility tool takes about two minutes and determines whether you may qualify for Italian citizenship by descent (Jure Sanguinis). We recommend using this tool before booking a call to ensure your time is well spent.

Intro Call

We walk you through our process, review your case, ask about documents you already have, outline additional requirements, and assess if our services align with your needs. If everything looks good, we’ll begin the Verification of Eligibility by collecting your documents.

Verification of Eligibility and Free Document Analysis

Once we receive your documents, our legal team will verify your eligibility for Italian citizenship, determine the best legal path forward, and provide a quote with an estimated timeline. We’ll then set up a quick onboarding call to review paperwork, payment details, and answer any additional questions you may have.

We Begin Your Application Submission

Once the paperwork is complete, we’ll promptly begin the process to obtain your citizenship. If proceeding through an Italian court, we’ll assemble your application and handle everything from Italy. If applying through a consulate, we’ll prepare your application and schedule your meeting. Throughout both processes, we’ll keep you informed at every milestone.

Pricing

Our pricing is based on your specific case, the number of documents we must obtain and process (translate, legalize, etc.), and whether it's a consulate or court case. Packages range from $750 - $11,000.
